Mohammad Nur Bramasta (b. August 15, 1997), commonly known by his friends as Bram or Bramasta is an undergraduate student of Universitas Hasanuddin, majoring in Electrical Engineering. He lives in Toddopuli, Makassar, Sulawesi Selatan, Indonesia.

Born in Banjarmasin from a Javanese father and Buginese mother, he traveled around Indonesia quite a lot in his childhood.

Introduced to Computers by his father early in childhood, he took interest and ultimately became addicted to programming, robotics and computer games.

Mastering C# is his current ambition.

Born into an adventurous family, Bram never stays in one place and culture. You may see this reflected by looking at his education history.
